Facilities

NIT Goa is providing hostel facilities to outstation students with shared accommodation in the campus and may be in a rented building which is very near to the campus. So far the existing hostels are provided with the basic amenities like mess and Wi-Fi internet connectivity. NIT Goa is having highly qualified faculty from IITs, NITs and reputed universities. The institute is functioning from its own campus consisting of sufficient class rooms and laboratories. We are also putting in additional effort to bring about new infrastructures into operation in near future. All the class rooms are modernized with projector and required electronic equipments. NIT Goa is having well established laboratories in all the existing branches and also continuously engaged in enriching it as per the academic requirements. We have the required hardware and software available with laboratories. Each of the laboratories is attended by Technicians and Technical assistants along with the concern faculty. We also ensure availability of required text books in the library. As a part of extra academic activities, NIT Goa has conducted several workshops in their respective departments to enlighten its students and also aims to continue it in future. NIT Goa is a new institution and is in its path of development, which is a matter of time.
