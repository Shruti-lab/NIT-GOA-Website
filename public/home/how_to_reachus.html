How to Reach Us ?

NIT Goa Campus is situated in Farmagudi, Ponda, Goa. The locality of Farmagudi rests atop a plateau, situated at a distance of 3 km from the central hub of Ponda City, along the route leading towards Panaji.

Ponda, encompassing both city and municipal council status, is positioned within the northern region of Goa's district and resides within the state of Goa, India. Placed centrally within Goa, the city of Ponda is situated approximately 28 km to the southeast of Panaji and about 17 km to the northeast of Margao.
